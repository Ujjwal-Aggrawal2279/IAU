About us
Imam Abdulrahman bin Faisal University is one of the pillars of higher education in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The University, with its specific, human and material resources, has a key role in scientific research, academic supervision and community service. The University has a wide base of qualified scientific and technical staff from the best local universities and a strong environment in all its faculties, scientific departments, research centers, laboratories, and modern information system.
The Institute of Consulting Studies at Imam Abdulrahman bin Faisal University is the link between the university, the public and private sectors and civil society institutions as a national consultancy, providing research, contractual studies, professional, academic and administrative consultations with high quality in line with the requirements of national development and Saudi labor market. And the requirements of the local community and work to achieve the vision of the Kingdom 2030 in building an ambitious homeland and a prosperous economy and a vibrant knowledge society developed through the vast material and human resource owned by the University.
